Short Courses

If you have the basic knowledge on photography and you want an in-depth knowledge on shooting a specific subject, short courses are right for you. You can enroll to one or more available short courses.

Landscape Photography

Appreciate more the beauty of the environment by learning the techniques on how to capture good landscape photos.

Duration: 6 weeks
Credential: Certificate
Price: $199.99

Human Photography

Take selfie photos to the next level. Learn to show the different facets of human emotions through photos.

Duration: 6 weeks
Credential: Certificate
Price: $199.99

Low light Photography

Take good photos in the dark by learning the techniques of low light photography.

Duration: 6 weeks
Credential: Certificate
Price: $199.99

Action Photography

Fast-moving subjects can be quiet a challenge. Learn the techniques of shutter speed and ISO.

Duration: 6 weeks
Credential: Certificate
Price: $199.99

Digital Photography

Improve your knowledge in DSLR operation. Learn the techniques of modern and computer-generated photography.

Duration: 6 weeks
Credential: Certificate
Price: $199.99

Architecture Photography

Capture photos of architectural designs focusing on different shapes, sizes, and dimensions.

Duration: 6 weeks
Credential: Certificate
