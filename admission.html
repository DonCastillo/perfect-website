Admission Process

Enrollment in DC Pixel School of Photography is an easy process. The steps to take are as follow:

  1. Choose which program package or short course(s) to take. Take a program package if you want a comprehensive knowledge and skill on photography. If you want a specialized knowledge on specific area of photography, choose any number if short courses you want.
  2. Fill out the Enroll Now application form.
  3. Wait at least 24 hours for us to receive your application. Once we received it, we will send you an email containing your student ID which you will use to sign-in to your Student Portal account. You can add password to your account.
  4. Now you are ready to learn.
