Curriculum for Program Packages

Basic Photography

HIST 1000
HISTORY OF PHOTOGRAPHY

Learn the history of photography and its evolution.

HIST 2000
OLD STYLE PHOTOGRAPHY

Know the different old style photography techniques and use of vintage cameras.

SUBJ 1000
SUBJECT IDENTIFICATION

Know wht subject to capture.

OPER 1260
BASICS OF CAMERA OPERATION

Learn how to operate different kinds of modern cameras like DSLR and point and shoot cameras.

OPER 1270
PRINCIPLES OF MODERN PHOTOGRAPHY

Understand the three aspects of photography: Aperture, Shutter Speed, ISO.

Intermediate Photography

SUBJ 2000
CHALLENGE IN PHOTOGRAPHY

Learn the different ways to shoot a subject in adverse or dangerous environments.

VISU 2510
VISUAL EXPOSURE

Learn different types of exposure and how it affects the overall image.

VISU 2520
VISUAL ELEMENTS 1

Manipulate camera settings based on subject's form, shape, texture, color, and line.

VISU 2530
VISUAL COMPOSITION 1

Explore techniques and practice of photography with emphasis in different shots or angles, balance, line of sight, depth of field, framing, perspectices, and pattern.
