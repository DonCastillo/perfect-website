Book for Photo Tour

What you have learned in the class is not enough without practice. DC Pixel School of Photography offers free photo tour to students enrolled in any program as part of their curriculum. Enthusiasts, hobbyists, and students enrolled in short courses are more than welcome. This is a rare opportunity to practice photography skills, travel the world, and have fun.

Travel Information


What is a travel code?
It is a 12-character alphanumeric code that determines all information about the tour: destination, accomodations, itinerary, and date of the tour.

For non students: visit the Contact page of the website or e-mail us at travelandtours@dcpixel.ca. The code is given after the interview by one of our agents.
